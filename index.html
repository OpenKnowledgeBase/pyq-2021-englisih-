Question 5: Write short notes (around 150 words each)

Characteristics of work ethics

Work ethics refer to a set of values and principles that guide an individual's behavior in the workplace. Key characteristics include honesty, integrity, responsibility, and professionalism. Employees with strong work ethics are punctual, reliable, and committed to their tasks. They demonstrate respect for colleagues and adhere to company policies. Work ethics also involve a positive attitude, teamwork, and a willingness to go the extra mile to achieve organizational goals. Employers value individuals with strong work ethics as they contribute to a productive and harmonious work environment.

Stages of presentation

A successful presentation typically involves several stages: planning, preparation, delivery, and follow-up. In the planning stage, the presenter identifies the purpose, audience, and key messages. Preparation involves researching the topic, creating visual aids, and rehearsing the presentation. During the delivery stage, the presenter engages the audience, maintains eye contact, and uses effective communication techniques. The follow-up stage includes addressing questions, gathering feedback, and reflecting on the presentation's success. Each stage is crucial for delivering a clear, impactful, and memorable presentation.
